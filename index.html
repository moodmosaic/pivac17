PIVAC-17

17th International Conference on Progress in Vaccination Against Cancer
Loutraki, Corinth, Greece

Announcement

We cordially invite you to the next PIVAC meeting "The 17th International Conference on Progress in Vaccination Against Cancer” (PIVAC-17) which will be held from 27th–30th September 2017 in Loutraki, Greece at the "Alexandrion" Conference Hall, located close to the Canal of Corinth.

The meeting will cover the most recent advances in the field of cancer immunotherapy with emphasis on cancer vaccines and retain an informal atmosphere for scientific discussions and interactions between speakers and participants.

We look forward to welcoming you to Loutraki.
